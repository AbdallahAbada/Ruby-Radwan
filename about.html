Ruby Radwan

LICENSED HYPNO PSYCHOTHERAPIST

I am a client centred holistic therapist

I understand that it is difficult to reach out for help. Daunting to share your problems and fears honestly with someone else. I have trained to be that person who can share your problems in order to help you discover your true inner strength. I am a non judgemental supportive therapist. I can help enable you to carry on with confidence, to make the changes necessary to give you real hope, self value and courage to change whatever you feel needs changing in your life.

I am an integrative therapist

My interest in hypnotherapy began over 30 years ago when I studied psychology at university. I went on to train as a holistic health practitioner, specialising in aromatherapy and reflexology. An in-depth interest in healthy eating was sparked when I became a mother which led to a much more holistic approach to health and disease. During my hypnotherapy training, I covered a range of different theoretical approaches; this has placed many useful techniques at my fingertips so I am able to focus on your unique needs rather than fit you into a particular theoretical box. I believe in a holistic approach to life, health and wellbeing. I believe the mind and body and spirit are so intricately and elegantly linked that healing can happen when you take the very first step.

MY MISSION

Finding Life Balance

Balance is not a destination, it is a constant journey.

When working to achieve perfection in life, we often set unrealistic and impossible expectations, setting ourselves up for disappointment and a sense of failure. By focusing on yourself and accepting the good in the present, I can help you change your mentality from disappointment with the impossible to acceptance and joy in real life by creating a true sense of being good enough. Because only when we stop setting outselves unrealistic expectations, and instead accept ourselves for who we are and see the good in our present selves, will we be able to achieve true balance in our lives.
