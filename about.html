Ruby Radwan

THERAPIST

I am a client-centered holistic therapist

As a client-centered holistic therapist, I acknowledge that seeking help can be challenging. Sharing your concerns and fears openly is a daunting task. My training equips me to be the person you can confide in, helping you uncover your inner strength. I approach therapy with non-judgmental support, aiming to empower you to move forward confidently and make the changes necessary to instill hope, self-worth, and the courage to transform aspects of your life that require change.

I am an integrative therapist

As an integrative therapist, I've cultivated a holistic perspective over 30 years. My journey began with psychology, followed by training as a holistic health practitioner, specializing in aromatherapy and reflexology. My interest in holistic health deepened when I became a parent, fostering a more comprehensive approach to well-being. My hypnotherapy training encompassed diverse theoretical approaches, providing me with a range of techniques to tailor therapy to your unique needs, rather than confining you to one method. I strongly believe in the interconnectedness of the mind, body, and spirit and how this connection can facilitate healing, starting with that crucial first step.

MY MISSION

Finding Life Balance

Balance is not a destination, it is a constant journey.

Striving for perfection can lead to unattainable goals and disappointment. I can guide you to shift your mindset from pursuing the impossible to finding contentment in the present.
